  • STUDENTS: Using Respondus Lockdown Browser for D2L Quizzes

    Many instructors require you to use Respondus Lockdown Browser (LDB) when taking D2L quizzes. This is a web browser, similar to Internet Explorer, Firefox, or Chrome, that you install on your computer. The only purpose of this browser is to access D2L quizzes that have been configured by your instructor to use it. When taking a D2L quiz in LDB, you will be unable to access other things on your laptop until you submit the quiz. 

    If you haven't installed LDB yet or you haven't updated the program recently, there are instructions in the Help menu. If you have any questons or problems, contact the Technical Support Center at techsupport@winona.edu or 507-457-5240 Option 1. 

  • Attention Students: Not seeing your classes in D2L?

    Posted Aug 25, 2015 2:13 PM

    If you are not seeing one or more of your classes in D2L, here are a few things to check:

    1. Make sure you are registered for the class. Check your official registration to make sure the class appears on your list. If you just Blue Carded into the course, give it a bit to run the process, maybe 48 hours. 
    2. If you are using Internet Explorer, there are known issues that prevent IE from displaying courses on your home page. You can either change the config of IE by following the steps listed in the News Item below or switch to another browser like Firefox.
    3. If you still cannot see a course, it is possible that your professor has not activated the course in D2L yet.
